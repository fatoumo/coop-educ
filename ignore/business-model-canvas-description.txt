Business Model Canvas - IT Apprenticeship Program
====================================================

This Business Model Canvas illustrates a comprehensive apprenticeship program model designed for the IT sector, focusing on work-study arrangements (alternance).

KEY COMPONENTS:

1. Key Partners (Partenaires Clés)
   - Training centers: CFA CESI/Simplon
   - Funding organizations: OPCO 2i
   - Regional government: Région Occitanie
   - Employer companies

2. Key Activities (Activités Clés)
   - Matching apprentices with companies
   - Tutor training programs
   - Educational monitoring
   - SaaS platform management

3. Value Proposition (Proposition Valeur)
   - Guaranteed first professional experience
   - Zero cost for participants
   - 3-month replacement guarantee
   - Intensive support throughout the program

4. Customer Relationships (Relation Client)
   - Dedicated account managers
   - Tutor hotline support
   - Online dashboard access
   - Community building

5. Customer Segments (Segments Clients)
   - Tech SMEs (10-50 employees)
   - Series A startups
   - Regional IT service companies
   - Career changers entering IT

6. Key Resources (Ressources Clés)
   - 3-person core team
   - Technology platform
   - Enterprise network
   - CFA certification

7. Channels (Canaux)
   - Direct B2B sales
   - Training center partnerships
   - Referral prescribers
   - Tech events and networking

8. Cost Structure (Structure Coûts)
   - Apprentice salaries: 85%
   - Operational structure: 10%
   - Technology infrastructure: 5%

9. Revenue Streams (Sources Revenus)
   - Public funding and subsidies: 70%
   - B2B services: 20%
   - SaaS subscriptions: 10%

This model leverages public funding to provide high-quality apprenticeship matching and support services while maintaining financial sustainability through diversified revenue streams.
